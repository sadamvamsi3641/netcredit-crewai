NETCREDIT LOAN POLICIES & COMPLIANCE GUIDELINES
================================================

OLA (Online Lenders Alliance) Guidelines
-----------------------------------------

OLA Rule 1 - APR Disclosure:
All lenders must disclose the Annual Percentage Rate (APR) 
clearly before the borrower signs any loan agreement. 
APR must be displayed prominently on the first page.

OLA Rule 2 - Cooling Off Period:
Borrowers have the right to cancel their loan within 
3 business days of signing without any penalty or fee.

OLA Rule 3 - Maximum Loan Amount:
Online lenders cannot issue loans exceeding $50,000 
to first-time borrowers without additional verification.

OLA Rule 4 - Income Verification:
All borrowers must provide proof of income before 
loan approval. Minimum income requirement is $1,500/month.

OLA Rule 5 - Credit Score Requirements:
Borrowers with credit scores below 500 must be rejected.
Borrowers between 500-600 require additional documentation.
Borrowers above 700 qualify for standard processing.

ILPA (Internet Lending Policy Act) Guidelines
----------------------------------------------

ILPA Rule 1 - Bankruptcy Check:
Any borrower with bankruptcy filed in the last 2 years 
must be automatically rejected regardless of credit score.

ILPA Rule 2 - Debt-to-Income Ratio:
Maximum allowed DTI ratio is 50% for loan approval.
DTI between 40-50% requires human analyst review.
DTI below 40% qualifies for automatic processing.

ILPA Rule 3 - Employment Verification:
Borrowers must be employed for minimum 6 months.
Self-employed borrowers need 2 years of tax returns.
Unemployed borrowers are automatically rejected.

ILPA Rule 4 - Data Privacy:
All borrower data must be encrypted and stored securely.
Borrower information cannot be shared with third parties.
Data retention period is maximum 7 years.

ILPA Rule 5 - Fair Lending:
Loan decisions must be based solely on financial criteria.
No discrimination based on race, gender, or location.
All rejected borrowers must receive written explanation.

NetCredit Internal Policies
----------------------------

Policy 1 - High Risk Applications:
Applications with credit score below 600 AND DTI above 40%
must be escalated to senior underwriter for review.

Policy 2 - Fraud Detection:
Any application showing unusual patterns must be flagged.
Multiple applications from same IP address are flagged.
Inconsistent income documentation triggers manual review.

Policy 3 - Loan Limits by Credit Tier:
LOW risk (score 700+): Up to $25,000 approved automatically
MEDIUM risk (score 600-699): Up to $10,000 with review
HIGH risk (score below 600): Maximum $5,000 with full review

Policy 4 - Processing Time:
Standard applications: 24 hour decision
High risk applications: 72 hour decision with human review
Emergency loans: Same day decision available for existing customers

Policy 5 - Interest Rates:
LOW risk borrowers: 8-15% APR
MEDIUM risk borrowers: 15-25% APR
HIGH risk borrowers: 25-36% APR (maximum allowed by law)
